I've always been facinated by the future. Whether through science fiction novels and television, or through watching presentations on what companies have planned for the future, the mystery of the future has always astounded me. Through attending university, I've become intent on finding ways to help create the future that techonology has helped me imagine.

Born in New York City, I'm a city boy at heart. I love cities and learning about what makes them tick. I keep fairly manicured google maps with my favorite restaurants in NYC and other cities that I happen upon. I'm always happy to share my discoveries!
