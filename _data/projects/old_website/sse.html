stanford student enterprises

intro.

In the summer of 2016, I had the opportunity to work as a graphic designer for Stanford Student Enterprises, working on material to be distributed to the Stanford community. SSE produces two major publications during the year, The Stanford Directory and The Unofficial Guide to Stanford University.


the products.

The Stanford Directory is distributed in Winter Quarter of every academic year to members of the Stanford community. SSE prints over 14,000 copies to be distributed- each copy a yellow pages for restaurants and businesses in the local area with coupons as well. The Unofficial Guide is distributed every Fall Quarter to incoming freshman and returning students. 13,500 copies are distributed university-wide. Enclosed are the "need-to-know" details about living at Stanford and enjoying the ride.

As the graphic designer I was given full creative control of the product, and was able to create a consisistent theme between the two items, strenghthening the overall brand of SSE's publications.



the theme.

Using photos from Stanford's archives, I sought to blend perspectives of the past with the present. 2016 marked Stanford University's 125th year, and members of the community kept Stanford's past, present, and future in front of mind. I saw this as an opportunity to enter that conversation, and bring together the Stanford of the past, with the Stanford of the presnet using photos from University Archives.
