stanford talisman.

Stanford Talisman is an a cappella group at Stanford University committed to the sharing of stories. Founded in 1989 out of the Anti-Apartheid movemment on campus, Stanford Talisman sings music from around the world, aiming to help share the stories of others.

stanford quad.

The Stanford Quad was Stanford's annual yearbook. I was able to work on the publication during the 2012-2014 years, designing layouts like these in coordination with a theme chosen by the Editor in Chief.
